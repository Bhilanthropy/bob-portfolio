Long-term scaled impact requires regular monthly giving. The compounding effect shown here assumes consistent contributions — every month you give, the endowment grows and generates more dividends for your causes. Irregular or one-time donations significantly slow the curve.

How your donations create impact

1
You donate monthly

Regular contributions are pooled into the 4H endowment. Consistency is essential — the model is built on sustained giving, not one-off gifts.

→
2
The endowment earns dividends

Invested in a dividend-focused equity portfolio (high-yield ETFs and market trackers), targeting ~5% annual dividend yield plus ~9% market appreciation. A pure S&P 500 index yields ~1.5% — 4H targets higher-yield strategies to maximise regular impact distributions.

→
3
Dividends are split 3 ways

50% reinvested to grow the endowment  ·  40% to your causes  ·  10% foundation operations (staff, compliance, platform)
